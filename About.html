Ambassadors exists to promote the communication of the gospel of Jesus and participate in the ministry of reconciling people to a relationship with God.  We desire to help those who want to serve in communities that generally have a transitional or transient nature.  Some of the communities that we desire to serve are college students, urban poor, the athletic community, and other nations.  Ambassadors provides a service for people who want to enter into these communities by helping to administrate and manage finances, as well as providing insight and resources.
